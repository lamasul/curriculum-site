The following activities are supplementary activities that kids should complete to reinforce these important concepts. These activities are listed in the order we suggest you follow.

  • Links: Color Matching Worksheet
  • Pre-Reqs: “Classifying vs. Generating AI” Slideshow
  • Suggested Length: 20 min
  • Tech Dependencies: Google Chrome
  • Key Concepts: Classifier, Generator
  • Group Reqs: None
  • Description: Students will classify colors against a color wheel and color chart.

  • Key Concepts: Classifier, Generator
